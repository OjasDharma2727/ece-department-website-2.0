About Us - ECE Department

Welcome to the ECE Department

The Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) Department at ABVGIET is dedicated to nurturing aspiring engineers by providing quality education and practical exposure. We emphasize both theoretical and hands-on learning to prepare students for industry challenges.

Our Head of Department

The department is led by Dr. Ankush Kapoor, an Associate Professor with vast experience in electronics and communication fields. Under his leadership, the department has excelled in both academics and research.

Intake and Admission

The department has an annual intake of 48 students. Admissions are made based on performance in national and state-level entrance examinations:

  • JEE (Joint Entrance Examination) - National Level
  • HPCET (Himachal Pradesh Common Entrance Test) - State Level
